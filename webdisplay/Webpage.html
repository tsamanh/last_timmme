Maintain Personal Privacy While Getting Help in School

We understand that your safety is the most important factor when it comes to working with new people. That is why Speed Study adheres to a strict privacy policy that minimizes the risk for outside users, and unwarranted student data collection

Profiles are voluntary and exclusive to UH students

Students are not required to set up a profile. However, once a student does through AccessUH, they should input relevant information regarding their coursework, to allow Speed Study to match them to the best resources available. Accounts can be set up with a peoplesoft ID, ensuring that only UH students will meet for study sessions.

Data is not shared with other students

Students have the option to input their a letter grade for certain classes, as well as feedback for different study groups. Data is shown on our webpage to let users view the overall efficacy of Speed Study, but user specific data is not shared with other students.

Meetings are held on campus only

Meetings can only be held in public spaces, such as classrooms, the library, or the student center. All study sessions must be approved by Speed Study, and students will be made aware of any suspicious activities.
